About

From India to Canada.
From clinical practice to clinical education.

I trained as a veterinarian in India and spent over four years in large-animal clinical practice — diagnosing, treating, and managing herds in real-world conditions. That experience shaped how I think about veterinary medicine: practical, patient-centered, and grounded in evidence.

Today, I live in Waterloo, Ontario, and work as a veterinary assistant/technician in Canada. I've passed the BCSE and NAVLE, and I'm actively continuing the Canadian veterinary licensing pathway. Every day in Canadian practice adds new clinical perspective to the foundation I built in India.

I started DVMReady because I saw a gap: international veterinary graduates need better tools to navigate licensing exams, clinical calculations, and the transition into North American practice. I build those tools myself — combining veterinary knowledge with modern web technology.
